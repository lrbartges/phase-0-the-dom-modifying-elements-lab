Set the text content of 'newHeader' to "YOUR-NAME is the champion" newHeader.textContent = "YOUR-NAME is the champion"; // Replace YOUR-NAME with your name // 5. Append 'newHeader' to the body of the document document.body.appendChild(newHeader); });
